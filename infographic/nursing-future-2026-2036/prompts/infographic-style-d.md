## Style Guidelines

### morandi-journal Aesthetic

Hand-drawn doodle illustration with warm Morandi color tones and cozy bullet journal aesthetic. Like a thoughtful nurse's personal study notebook.

### Color Palette (strict)

- **Background**: Warm cream/beige with subtle paper texture (#F5F0E6) — never pure white
- **Primary**: Muted teal/sage green (#7BA3A8) for headers, frames, badge backgrounds
- **Secondary**: Warm terracotta (#D4956A) for highlights, key numbers, important callouts
- **Accent (highlight quadrant)**: Soft dusty purple (#A89AB2) for the "Digital Care Integrator" star quadrant
- **Caution (risk quadrant)**: Soft amber/sand (#E5C9A0) for the "Tech-Enabled Assistant" warning quadrant
- **Line art**: Dark charcoal brown (#4A4540) — slightly varied line weight, not uniform
- **Soft highlights**: Pale yellow (#F5E6C8) for washi tape and corner decorations

### Visual Elements (required)

- **Hand-drawn doodle illustrations** with organic, slightly imperfect ink lines
- **Washi tape strips** (diagonal stripes, beige/brown) decorating module corners
- **Rounded card containers** for the 4 quadrant tiles and 5 recommendation cards
- **Hand-drawn rulers, scales, progress bars** with quality indicators
- **Smiley/frowny face markers** (😊 for "future role", 😐 for traditional, ☹️ for at-risk)
- **Dotted line frames** around each module (organic, not strict)
- **Connecting arrows and dotted lines** between modules (e.g., from drivers to quadrants)
- **Corner decorations**: tiny stars ✦, sparkles, clouds, small crosses ✚
- **Wavy line dividers** between sections
- **Callout bubbles** for tips and warnings
- **Hand-drawn thumbs up/down icons** (✓ ✗) for quadrants
- **Small magnifying glass** icon on identification/tips modules
- **Hand-drawn avatar silhouettes** of nurses for each quadrant (different poses)
- **Subtle paper texture** overlay across the entire background

### Typography

- **Main title**: Bold hand-lettered calligraphy style with decorative flourishes — "อนาคตของวิชาชีพพยาบาล 2026-2036"
- **Subtitle**: Lighter handwritten script — "The Future of Nursing | ทักษะ บทบาท และทิศทางใหม่"
- **Module headers**: Clean handwritten text in white on dark teal rounded badge
- **Body text**: Neat handwritten print, easy to read, multiple columns within modules
- **Numbers**: Highlighted in terracotta (#D4956A), slightly larger than body
- **Bilingual labels**: Thai primary size, English secondary smaller (parenthetical or below)

### Style Enforcement (strict)

- All imagery must maintain hand-drawn/doodle aesthetic — no digital precision
- Organic, slightly imperfect shapes throughout (asymmetry is welcome)
- Sketch-like quality with visible line weight variations
- Warm and cozy journal feel, not clinical or corporate
- Like a thoughtful nursing leader's personal study notebook, not a hospital poster

### AVOID (strict)

- Flat vector icons or emoji faces
- Clean geometric shapes (perfect circles, perfect squares)
- Stock illustration style
- Strict grid layout
- Pure white background
- Digital/corporate look
- Neon colors, gradients, glow effects
- Photorealistic imagery
- Clinical/medical infographic clichés (stethoscopes with perfect shine, etc.)
